About Me

Technical Game Designer

I'm a passionate Technical Game Designer with a Bachelor's degree in Game Design from Full Sail University. Currently working as a Gameplay Programmer Intern at T'sarE LLC, I specialize in Unreal Engine 5, Blueprint scripting, and C++ development.

My approach combines creative design thinking with technical implementation skills, allowing me to bridge the gap between vision and reality. I'm driven by the challenge of creating immersive player experiences through innovative gameplay systems, responsive mechanics, and thoughtful level design.

When I'm not designing levels or prototyping mechanics, I'm playtesting games, studying player psychology, and exploring new technologies in the game development space.
