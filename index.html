Project Overview

Shadowborne was my comprehensive capstone project at Full Sail University, demonstrating advanced gameplay design and QA methodologies. The project focused on creating immersive combat experiences with responsive systems and environmental storytelling, showcasing my ability to bridge the gap between design vision and technical implementation.

As both the Gameplay Designer/Programmer and QA Lead, I was responsible for designing level layouts, implementing gameplay systems using Blueprint and C++, and ensuring quality through rigorous testing and documentation. The project emphasized player engagement through precision-timed combat mechanics and adaptive AI systems.

Key Contributions

  • Designed immersive level layouts emphasizing environmental storytelling, pacing, and player engagement
  • Implemented responsive UI/UX healing systems and precision-timed combat mechanics using Blueprint and C++
  • Developed adaptive enemy AI systems to enhance gameplay variety and challenge progression
  • Led QA documentation through Jira for comprehensive issue tracking and cross-team coordination
  • Conducted iterative playtesting sessions to refine mechanics and ensure quality player experiences

Project Overview

An adventure-themed level design project inspired by Indiana Jones and Tomb Raider, focusing on creating intuitive navigation, environmental storytelling, and cohesive team collaboration. This project emphasized player-driven exploration through clever use of landmarks and visual cues.

As part of a team of designers, I was responsible for creating my own section of a larger connected level while maintaining consistency with the overall adventure theme. The project required careful asset selection, puzzle design, and use of source control for seamless team integration.

Key Contributions

  • Designed gate and valve puzzle systems optimizing player progression, flow, and navigation clarity
  • Implemented Blueprint-based mechanics using environmental storytelling, landmarks, and leading lines
  • Utilized Perforce source control for team collaboration and seamless asset integration
  • Managed asset sourcing and integration from Epic Games Marketplace while maintaining theme consistency
  • Conducted peer playtesting to gather feedback and refine level design decisions
