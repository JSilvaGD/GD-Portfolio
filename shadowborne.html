Project Overview

Shadowborne was my comprehensive capstone project at Full Sail University, demonstrating advanced gameplay design and QA methodologies. The project focused on creating immersive combat experiences with responsive systems and environmental storytelling, showcasing my ability to bridge the gap between design vision and technical implementation.

As both the Gameplay Designer/Programmer and QA Lead, I was responsible for designing level layouts, implementing gameplay systems using Blueprint and C++, and ensuring quality through rigorous testing and documentation. The project emphasized player engagement through precision-timed combat mechanics and adaptive AI systems.

My Contributions

  • Designed immersive level layouts emphasizing environmental storytelling, pacing, and player engagement
  • Implemented responsive UI/UX healing systems using Blueprint and C++ for seamless player feedback
  • Developed precision-timed combat mechanics that reward player skill and timing
  • Created adaptive enemy AI systems that scale difficulty based on player performance
  • Led QA documentation through Jira, tracking bugs and coordinating fixes across development cycles
  • Conducted iterative playtesting sessions with peers and gathered actionable feedback
  • Optimized performance across multiple gameplay systems to maintain consistent frame rates

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Balancing combat difficulty to maintain player engagement without frustration.

Solution: Implemented an adaptive AI system that analyzes player performance metrics and adjusts enemy behavior dynamically. This included enemy health scaling, attack frequency adjustments, and strategic positioning based on player skill level.

Challenge: Managing QA workflow as a solo developer while maintaining development momentum.

Solution: Established a structured Jira workflow with priority categorization, scheduled dedicated testing sessions, and created comprehensive bug reproduction documentation to streamline the fixing process.

What I Learned

This project taught me the importance of balancing creative vision with technical constraints. Working as both designer and QA lead gave me a holistic understanding of the development pipeline and the critical role that iterative testing plays in refining gameplay experiences.

I also gained valuable experience in C++ optimization, Blueprint architecture, and the importance of clear documentation when working on complex systems. The project reinforced my passion for creating responsive, player-focused gameplay that feels satisfying and rewarding.
